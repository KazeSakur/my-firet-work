Worldview

The plot begins

In December 1096, the Integration Movement, the world's largest organization of infected people, started a riot in the Imperial city of Chernoberg. They took advantage of a natural disaster to capture the city and prepared to use it as a springboard to attack the nearby city's Dragon Gate. At the same time, another mysterious infected group, Rhode Island, has appeared here, about to come into direct conflict with the integration movement. The story of tomorrow's Ark also begins from here...

Terra

For the vast majority, if not all, of people living in this land, Terra as a home is too cruel. In addition to the haze-like ore disease and the natural disasters that cause landslides and landslides, in the territory of known civilizations, countless inhuman forces lurking in the snow, desert, and deep sea are eyeing the tiger. The accompanying disasters, the endless struggles between countries, the dark secrets hidden for thousands of years, and the primitive malice beyond sight constitute the most cruel and desperate background of the Terra world.
